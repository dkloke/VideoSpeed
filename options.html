VideoSpeed

by Dan Kloke ©2019 - MIT / Google

from a tweet by @kentcdodds

This extension looks for all <video> elements on the page, and adjusts their playback speed (.playbackRate) simultaneously for all tags on the page.

Adjust video playback rate using ↑/↓ cursor keys, a mouse/touch interface, or manual entry.

The app does not send any information to the developer, and only stores user preferences locally. VideoSpeed makes no effort to be compatible with on-page code or other browser apps.

Repeated speed adjustments can cause video players to lose audio synchronization. To recover audio sync, try skipping forward/back or adjusting the video's replay position, stop and restart the player, or reload the page.

When this setting is disabled, VideoSpeed tries to acquire the current player speed (usually 1, unless previously adjusted with VideoSpeed).
